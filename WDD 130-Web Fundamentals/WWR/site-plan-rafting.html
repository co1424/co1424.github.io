Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to give you the best opportunity to learn and practice WHITE WATER RAFTING! Here you will find the right equipment, trainers, and more! We want to teach you how to be safe, and help others be safe while you practice this exciting sport. You will find personal experiences from people who decided to embark in this fun adventure. This is a family sport if you want it to be just that, or a friend related activity to bond with others.

Audience

People with the physical capabilities to hold on during the rapids and be willing to get wet! Outdoorsy people will be encouraged to bring their friends and family over. We also welcome people who want to do this for the first time ever, and those who want to do it more often. People will be able to access our site from their mobile devices and laptop.
